Course Learning Objectives 1. Apply and critically analyze theoretical models of Recreation Therapy practice; 2. Apply the assessment, program development, implementation and evaluation cyclical model of Recreation Therapy practice; 3. Develop the skills and knowledge base to confidently participate in standardized testing required by professional regulatory bodies in Recreation Therapy; 4. Experience and apply the domains of Leisure and Recreation; 5. Describe and apply the twelve Standards of Practice promoted by Therapeutic Recreation Ontario to recreation therapy practice; 6. Critically review and discuss theoretical constructs and contemporary thinking in leadership
